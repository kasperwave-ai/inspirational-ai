## Welcome

This book is for **high-achieving high schoolers** aiming for competitions like
[USAAIO](https://www.usaaio.org), students preparing science fair projects using AI,
and college freshmen and sophomores who want to go beyond surface-level exposure and
actually *build* intelligent systems.

Every chapter follows the same philosophy borrowed from [fast.ai](https://fast.ai):

1. **Code first** — a complete, runnable program before any theory
2. **Wonder second** — what just happened, and why is it surprising?
3. **Theory third** — the math and concepts, grounded in what you already ran

No prerequisites beyond basic Python. No hand-waving. No black boxes.

---

## How to Use This Book

**To read:** navigate the chapters on the left.

**To run the code:** every chapter has a launch button at the top —
click it to open the notebook in Google Colab and run it instantly,
no installation required.

**To go deeper:** each chapter ends with exercises, including at least
one ⭐ challenge problem suitable for a science fair or competition submission.
